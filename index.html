Our Story

Serving Kisii with Dedication

Asati-Anyona & Co. Advocates is an established law firm based in Kitutu Central, Kisii. The firm has built a strong reputation across land law, civil litigation, family matters and succession proceedings, with court records demonstrating an active litigation practice across the High Court at Kisii, the Environment and Land Court, and the Court of Appeal.

The firm is committed to delivering precise, professional legal services to every client — treating each matter with the care and dedication it deserves.
